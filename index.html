About us

EyeGuard is a mobile app and a non-profit called EyeGuard Impact Inc. It is designed to detect early signs of common eye conditions such as nearsightedness, digital eye strain, and color blindness through interactive vision tests. Built using Flutter and Android Studio, it offers users a series of scientifically backed screening tools, like the eye chart exam, contrast sensitivity test, and eye fatigue questionnaire, that assess visual health in minutes. Beyond testing, EyeGuard helps users form healthy screen habits through reminders, personalized reports, and easy-to-understand results. The app combines technology and awareness to make early eye health monitoring accessible for everyone, aiming to reduce preventable vision problems through education, detection, and daily care.
